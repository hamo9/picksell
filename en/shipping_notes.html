Stages of changing the application on the site after registration

The receipt of the request

Within 24 hours of registering the order, the confirmation team confirms the order through a confirmation call with the customer, in which only the customer’s data and product name are confirmed. Confirmation of the order on the same day to go out for delivery on the next day, and in the case of registration after 7 pm, it will be confirmed on the next day to go out for delivery on the next day.

Request suspended

In the event that the first confirmation call is not answered, the customer will be tried again on the same day. In the event of no response both times, a third and fourth attempt will be made on the next day until a response is made. In the event of no response, a fifth, sixth and seventh attempt will be made on the third day. Suspension of the application after the expiry of all seven attempts.

Order confirmed

After confirming the order, the date of receiving the customer will be calculated according to the governorate to which he belongs, and the official dates for delivery: Cairo, Giza and Alexandria within two working days, Lower Egypt and Qalyubia governorates within three working days, Upper Egypt, Sinai, Matrouh and the Red Sea governorates from two to five working days.

Waiting for the shipping company

The shipment is being prepared and waiting for the shipping company to receive the product from our warehouse

Delivery

The order has been delivered to the shipping company and will reach the customer during the official shipping dates, and the representative will communicate with the customer to set an appropriate date and day.

Delivery suspension

The order is suspended in several cases, namely, the customer does not respond to the calls of the shipping company representative or there is any problem in the address, or the delivery is postponed through the customer, and the order is temporarily suspended until the address is clarified or another number is sent from the merchant to us, or on the day specified in it The receipt.

Cancellation or rejection of the order

In the event that the customer rejects the request or cancels it for any reason, the status of the request is changed to “Rejected” with the reason being clarified in the notes and the profits of the request are not added to your wallet.

Delivered

The profit of the order appears in the expected profit box within 6-12 hours at most from receiving the customer, and becomes exchangeable within 24 hours from the date of receipt.

Return, Replacement and Refund Policy

A merchant has facilitated the after-sales service through a direct WhatsApp number that your customer can contact in case of a request for exchange and return, all you have to do is add this direct WhatsApp number 01013785551 to your store / page and ads (please note that the number works on WhatsApp only) and the exchange / refund will be done when Communicate with your customer through it without worrying about it.

Note :

In case of redemption, the profit will not be suspended.

In the event of a return, the amount will be transferred to the cash number shown in the order 72 hours after the product reaches the stores, in order to inspect the product and ensure its condition, in the event that there is no defect or problem with the product, 30% of the paid value will be deducted

Exchange and profit withdrawal requests

The merchant can withdraw the exchangeable profits on any day of the week, and the profits are transferred after 6 - 12 working hours after 24 hours have passed from confirming the status of the order to the delivery to the customer, and the withdrawal process is done through any number with a cash service (Vodafone - Etisalat - Orange-way), a bank wallet, or by bank transfer, with a minimum of 3000 pounds.

Tips to increase delivery rates and speed of delivery

Make sure the customer data (binary name and phone number) add another phone number to increase the possibility of answering the confirmation call and the representative.

Please inform all customers to wait for a confirmation call from a strange number of the shipping company to confirm the order and then respond to the representative upon delivery and that the mobile phone is available to respond.

Please make sure that there is a clear address (the governorate-area-street-building-floor-apartment) if it is a sub-street, please mention the main street with a distinctive sign that helps in speeding up the arrival of the representative to the place and increasing the rates of delivery.

Please enter the orders as soon as they are requested by the customer and do not postpone them to ensure their speedy arrival and non-cancellation.

Confirmation with the customer that the experiment is not allowed with the representative and that the order receives an invoice as a guarantee of the product, and in the event of a defect, it will be replaced or returned within 14 days.

Confirm the final price including shipping with the final customer to ensure delivery.

Accuracy in presenting the product details to the customer so that the customer does not reject the product when seeing the product on the ground.

Please follow up on updates of order statuses permanently through the site and inform us through messages in the event of any new update from the customer to ensure the confirmation and delivery of the customer.

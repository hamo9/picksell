Exchange and Completion Policy

The merchant submits a request for replacement, retrieval or completion to a merchant company in the event that the final customer has a complaint about the product, by opening the site, logging in to the account, accessing the request, and then pressing the replace, retrieval or completion button, and the request is answered within 24 hours a job

In the case of approval, the representative will reach the customer within 3-5 working days, and in case of rejection, the reason for rejection will be written in the tracking of the request

In the event of a return, the amount will be transferred to the cash number shown in the order 72 hours after the product reaches the stores in order to inspect the product and confirm its condition, in the event that there is no defect or problem with the product, 30% of the paid value will be deducted
